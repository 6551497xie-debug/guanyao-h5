## 1. Locked Definition

> 六维 = 生命体验现实世界的六个界面。

The six spaces are the six life experience interfaces through which the starbeast, as the user's Life Archetype, experiences reality:

```text
身体 → 情绪 → 思想 → 行动 → 记忆 → 动机
```

They describe one complete life moving through reality. They do not divide a person into six separate parts.

The six spaces are not:

- six personality categories;
- six user problems;
- a psychological diagnosis;
- a score, level, or assessment report;
- six treatment or repair modules;
- a task checklist used to judge completion.

GUANYAO uses the six spaces to help the user notice how an experience is passing through life, not to tell the user what is wrong with them.

## 2. The Six Life Experience Interfaces

### Body | 身体

The body is how reality is first felt through breath, tension, warmth, fatigue, posture, pace, and other embodied sensations.

Body signals are not evidence that the body is broken. They are one way life makes the present moment perceivable.

### Emotion | 情绪

Emotion is how experience forms an inner wave: joy, fear, anger, grief, calm, longing, or an unnamed shift.

Emotion is a lived signal, not a verdict about the user and not a conclusion that must be corrected immediately.

### Thought | 思想

Thought is how life interprets reality through judgment, explanation, anticipation, and narrative.

A thought is an interpretation occurring within experience. It is not identical to reality itself.

### Action | 行动

Action is how life approaches, pauses, leaves, protects, creates, or responds in reality.

GUANYAO does not use this space to correct behavior into a standard answer. It helps the user notice the direction already being taken and whether another response is possible.

### Memory | 记忆

Memory is how the past returns to the present as reference, echo, image, bodily familiarity, or expectation.

Memory does not rename the present as the past. It lets the user see what earlier experience is accompanying the current moment.

### Motivation | 动机

Motivation is what life truly wants to protect, seek, approach, sustain, or create beneath the visible response.

It is not a performance target or an external KPI. It is the direction in which life is trying to move.

## 3. One Experience, Six Interfaces

One environment force may be experienced through all six interfaces at the same time:

```text
环境力量
→ 身体有所感受
→ 情绪产生波动
→ 思想形成解释
→ 行动出现方向
→ 记忆回到此刻
→ 动机试图守住或寻找某物
```

This is not a mandatory linear order. It is a readable expression of one whole experience.

No single interface can define the whole user. No interface is inherently more advanced, more healthy, or more complete than another.

## 4. Primary Petal Boundary

Primary Petal identifies the life interface from which the current experience can be observed most clearly and gently.

It means:

> 这一刻，可以先从这里看见自己。

Primary Petal is not:

- the user's most damaged dimension;
- the user's weakest personality trait;
- a diagnosis or permanent identity;
- proof that the other five interfaces are absent;
- a new source of Mother Code, starbeast, upper trigram, or Crystal.

The existing deterministic mapping and fallback behavior remain unchanged. This protocol only calibrates their product meaning.

## 5. Six-Node Experience Rhythm

The six nodes are an observation rhythm within the current life interface. They are not the six spaces themselves.

At the worldview level, the rhythm allows the user to:

1. notice what is happening;
2. pause the automatic response;
3. allow one small new response;
4. stay with the emerging experience;
5. perceive what has changed;
6. let this experience settle and continue into the next transition.

The nodes must not be presented as:

- task check-in;
- treatment, cure, or personality repair;
- defeating pressure or eliminating a black hole;
- pass/fail evaluation;
- proof that the user has completed psychological growth.

The existing node names, order, progress data, and completion rules remain runtime compatibility facts. Their completion state marks an engineering boundary in the current experience; it does not declare that the user has been fixed or has finished becoming.

## 6. Relationship To Gravity And Choice

Gravity is the pull of reality as it is experienced through the six interfaces. The six spaces make that pull observable; they do not frame the user as defective.

Choice remains with the user. GUANYAO may open a pause, reflect a pattern, and make another direction visible, but it must not choose on the user's behalf.

When a new response is genuinely experienced, it may enter the ChangeExperience chain. Node completion alone must not fabricate a ChangeExperience.

## 7. Relationship To Crystal

The six-space experience is not Crystal.

Six-node completion only allows the current experience to continue through the established migration and Crystal chain. Crystal is the life imprint left after change, not a score awarded for completing six nodes.

```text
六维生命体验
→ 用户觉察与选择
→ ChangeExperience
→ PersonaMigrationImpact
→ CrystalState
→ HexagramCrystalResult
→ 最终生命印记表达
```

This semantic chain does not replace the current engine contracts or authorize a new output path.

## 9. Front-Stage Language Boundary

Preferred direction:

- “先看看，这股力量怎样经过你的身体。”
- “这个念头正在怎样解释眼前的事？”
- “过去的哪一段经验，也来到了这一刻？”
- “你真正想守住、寻找或创造的是什么？”
- “不用急着改变，先感受它正在怎样发生。”

Avoid:

- “系统正在分析你的六维人格。”
- “你的这个维度存在问题。”
- “完成任务即可修复当前状态。”
- “这一维能量不足。”
- “你需要清除旧模式才能通关。”
- “主修花瓣代表你最薄弱的部分。”
