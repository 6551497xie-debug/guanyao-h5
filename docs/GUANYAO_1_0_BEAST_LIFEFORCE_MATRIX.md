# GUANYAO 1.0 Beast Lifeforce Matrix

This document freezes the Beast Lifeforce feedback matrix for GUANYAO 1.0.

It defines how cosmic time, starbeast emotional state, particle physics, haptic tone, and user-facing narrative should align. This is a product surface contract and a future implementation reference. It does not change runtime code.

## 1. Spacetime Clock Matrix

The starfield should feel alive across the day, but never unstable, punishing, or noisy. Local time may later be used to select the cosmic phase.

| Phase | Time Window | Cosmic Aesthetic State | Baseline Particle Velocity | Color Spectrum | Starbeast Baseline Mood |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Dawn | 05:00-11:00 | Soft emergence, mist-like particles, low horizon glow | 0.35x-0.55x, upward drift | pale gold, morning blue, warm white | waking guardian |
| Noon | 11:00-17:00 | Clear field, stable light, high legibility | 0.45x-0.70x, radial breathing | clean white, soft cyan, restrained gold | alert companion |
| Dusk | 17:00-23:00 | Warm compression, amber dust, quiet gravity | 0.30x-0.50x, inward spiral | amber gold, violet dusk, bone white | protective witness |
| Night | 23:00-05:00 | Deep field, slow stars, high silence | 0.15x-0.35x, slow suspension | deep indigo, cold gold, dim silver | silent shield |

### Clock Rules

- Dawn helps users start again.
- Noon supports clarity without pressure.
- Dusk holds emotional transition with warmth.
- Night protects the user from overstimulation.
- No phase may imply punishment, failure, or depletion.

## 2. Starbeast Emotion Matrix

The starbeast expresses relation to the pressure field, not judgment of the user.

## 3. Finch High-Stickiness Principles

The system should keep users close through warmth, not pressure.

- The starbeast never punishes the user.
- Incompletion is not failure.
- The blackhole is an external disturbance field, not user guilt.
- The starbeast is a shield, not a pet burdened by the user.
- Micro-actions must still produce positive feedback.
- The smallest touch can return a little light.
- The user should feel accompanied before they feel challenged.
- The product may be precise, but the ending must be humane.

## 4. Technical Landing Suggestions

These are future implementation suggestions only. They are not part of this document change.

- Resolve starbeast emotion state from pressure seed and six-node progress.

### useBeastPhysicsMatrix

### Local Time Based Cosmic Phase

Future mapping:

- local time -> Dawn / Noon / Dusk / Night
- phase -> particle velocity / color spectrum / baseline mood

### Emotion State Based Particle Uniforms

Future mapping:

- STRAINED -> lower cohesion, contained turbulence
- CHARGING -> rising cohesion, warmer highlight
- SOVEREIGN -> settled cohesion, calm glow

### Haptic Feedback

Future rules:

- Haptics are optional.
- Haptics must never block flow.
- Haptics must never communicate punishment.
- Confirmation should feel like being held, not being scored.

## 5. Forbidden Patterns

The following are explicitly forbidden in GUANYAO 1.0 Beast Lifeforce expression:

- Do not use starbeast dimming to punish the user.
- Do not use red failure states to stimulate anxiety.
- Do not use copy such as "任务失败", "能量不足", or "你没有完成".
- Do not present the pressure seed as the user's mistake.
- Do not turn BaiHu into a cold, hard decorative graphic.
- Do not make the blackhole feel like shame.
- Do not make the starbeast feel disappointed in the user.
- Do not replace companionship with diagnosis.

## 6. Product Contract

Beast Lifeforce V1 exists to keep one promise:

The system can reveal pressure, but it must not abandon the user inside that pressure.

The starbeast is the user's companion through the field. It protects, receives, and reflects small returning light. Every future visual, haptic, or language implementation must preserve that emotional contract.
