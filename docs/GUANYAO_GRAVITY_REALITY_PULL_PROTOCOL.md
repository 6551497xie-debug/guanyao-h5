## 1. Locked Definition

> Gravity = 现实世界作用于生命的引力。

Gravity describes the pull that external standards, evaluation, pressure, algorithms, relationships, scarcity, uncertainty, and familiar responses can exert on a life.

It is the experience of being repeatedly pulled away from direct feeling, inner direction, and the ability to choose.

Gravity is not:

- fate or a fixed life result;
- a defect inside the user;
- proof that the user is weak;
- a psychological diagnosis;
- a hostile force that must be defeated;
- a score of how much lifeforce remains;
- the upper trigram, current hexagram, Choice, or Crystal.

Gravity is a relationship that can be observed. It is not an identity assigned to the user.

## 2. Why Gravity Exists In GUANYAO

People are continually shaped by systems outside themselves:

- other people's expectations;
- social measures of success;
- relationship roles and obligations;
- scarcity, loss, conflict, and uncertainty;
- algorithmic attention and comparison;
- responses that once protected life and later became familiar paths.

These forces do not make a person defective. But when their pull becomes difficult to notice, a person may begin to treat external demands or automatic responses as the only possible direction.

GUANYAO does not use Gravity to expose a flaw. It uses Gravity to make the pull observable, so the user can regain contact with feeling, interpretation, and choice.

## 3. The Meaning Of “Forgetting”

The V2 worldview says that reality's pull may cause the starbeast to gradually forget who it is.

“Forgetting” is a life-experience metaphor. It means that the user may temporarily lose access to:

- what the body is already feeling;
- what an emotion is trying to signal;
- the distinction between thought and reality;
- the ability to pause before acting;
- the difference between past experience and the present;
- the direction life truly wants to protect, seek, or create.

It does not mean that the starbeast has disappeared, that lifeforce has been damaged, or that the user has lost an essential self.

The starbeast remains present. Gravity changes how clearly life can feel and choose; it does not remove the life itself.

## 4. Pressure Seed, PressureField, And Gravity

These three concepts must remain distinct.

### Pressure Seed | 环境力量入口

Pressure Seed answers:

> 此刻，什么现实处境正在进入这一局？

It carries the recognizable event, relationship, environment, and semantic material used by the existing pressure classification chain.

### PressureField | 作用范围与强度

PressureField answers:

> 这股力量在当前窗口中以怎样的强度、时长与范围发生？

It carries engineering fields such as field weight, risk window, and `gravityValue`.

### Gravity | 被生命体验到的牵引

Gravity answers:

> 这股现实力量正在怎样牵引我的感受、解释、反应与方向？

Gravity is the product-level experience made visible through the six spaces. It is not a replacement for Pressure Seed or PressureField and does not introduce a new calculation layer.

```text
现实处境
→ Pressure Seed 形成可辨认入口
→ PressureField 表达当前作用范围与强度
→ Gravity 作为现实牵引被生命体验
→ 六维让这种体验可以被看见
```

This chain is a semantic reading of existing data. It does not authorize a second causal engine.

## 5. Relationship To Mother Code And Starbeast

Mother Code is the original life tendency carried into reality. It does not cause pressure and does not make the user deserve Gravity.

Reality may pull the same original tendency into different expressions. A natural capacity to connect, protect, advance, observe, or hold may become narrowed when only one response appears available.

This does not make the original tendency wrong. Gravity reveals how a living strength is currently being pulled, not which Mother Code is defective.

The starbeast is the Life Archetype experiencing this pull. It is not a combat unit sent to defeat Gravity, and Gravity does not generate or change the user's starbeast identity.

## 6. Relationship To The Six Spaces

Gravity is experienced through the six life interfaces:

| Life Interface | How Reality's Pull May Be Experienced |
| --- | --- |
| 身体 | 呼吸、紧绷、疲惫、停住、加速或失去身体感 |
| 情绪 | 一种波动被放大、压下或直接当作结论 |
| 思想 | 某种解释反复出现，并逐渐像唯一现实 |
| 行动 | 自动靠近、回避、推进、迎合、控制或冻结 |
| 记忆 | 过去的经验回到此刻，影响对现实的预期 |
| 动机 | 外部评价遮住真正想守住、寻找或创造的方向 |

These are not six separate Gravity engines and not six diagnoses.

Primary Petal only identifies the current observation entrance. It does not locate the most damaged space, and it does not imply that the other five interfaces are unaffected.

The six nodes create an observation rhythm inside that entrance. Completing them does not eliminate Gravity or certify that the user has overcome reality.

## 7. Black Hole And Visual Metaphor Boundary

The black hole may remain as a visual metaphor for reality's pull, compression, distance, distortion, or loss of direction.

It must not be presented as:

- a wound or defect inside the user;
- an enemy that the starbeast must kill;
- a force created by the user's failure;
- an object that must be filled before life can continue;
- proof that unfinished interaction has reduced the user's energy;
- a threat that grows in order to punish disengagement.

Visual intensity must express the current experience, not the user's worth.

The starbeast may appear quiet, distant, compressed, slowed, or partially obscured, but it must not be shown as losing its essential life because the user has not completed an interaction.

They may continue to influence the established risk window, transmission tone, or intervention intensity. They do not mean:

- six levels of user weakness;
- six levels of starbeast damage;
- six levels of spiritual growth;
- six possible upper trigrams;
- six final outcomes.

## 9. Relationship To Choice

Gravity makes the current pull visible. Choice belongs to the user.

Choice may be:

- pausing before an automatic response;
- staying with a feeling without immediately obeying it;
- asking whether an interpretation is the whole reality;
- taking one small different action;
- deciding not to act yet;
- declining, leaving, or returning later.

The system may reflect what is happening and make possible directions visible. It must not force an “anti-instinct” answer, preselect the correct direction, or treat continued pressure as failure.

Gravity may still exist after Choice. The meaningful change is that the user is no longer required to experience its pull as the only possible direction.

This protocol does not create or modify a Choice Runtime.

## 10. Relationship To ChangeExperience And Crystal

Seeing Gravity does not by itself prove that change occurred.

Only a user-recognized or explicitly confirmed new response may enter the established ChangeExperience path. Runtime completion alone must not fabricate that response.

```text
Gravity 被看见
→ 用户保有或重新取得选择空间
→ 新回应被真实确认
→ ChangeExperience
→ PersonaMigrationImpact
→ CrystalState
→ 最终生命印记表达
```

Crystal is not a reward for defeating Gravity. It is the life imprint left by an experience in which something was seen and a different relationship to the pull became possible.

## 11. Front-Stage Language Boundary

Preferred direction:

- “有一股现实力量，正在把你拉向熟悉的方向。”
- “先不用对抗它，看看这种牵引正在怎样发生。”
- “这个反应也许曾经保护过你。”
- “压力还在，但它不一定只有一种回应。”
- “你可以停一下，再决定要不要沿着这条路继续。”

Avoid:

- “系统发现了你的人格缺陷。”
- “你的重力值过高，说明你太脆弱。”
- “黑洞正在吞噬你的生命能量。”
- “完成六维即可消除负面惯性。”
- “你必须战胜压力，星兽才能恢复。”
- “AI 已经判断出你的正确选择。”
- “未完成将导致黑洞继续扩大。”

Front-stage language should invite observation and preserve uncertainty. It must not intensify fear to create engagement.

## 12. Visual And Emotional Direction

Gravity should feel like a perceivable pull in space, not a battle against evil.

Suitable visual expressions include:

- orbital drift;
- light bending or narrowing;
- changes in distance, pace, breath, and posture;
- dust, tide, pressure, or field distortion;
- a quiet loss and recovery of orientation.

Avoid excessive injury, aggression, punishment, depletion meters, enemy-health metaphors, or victory explosions.

The visual objective is:

> 我第一次看见，原来这股力量一直在这样拉着我。
