## 1. Locked Definition

> 卦象 = 一次生命经历中，力量如何相遇、变化如何发生的结构表达。

Hexagram gives form to a relationship:

```text
生命携带的原始倾向
×
当前遇到的环境力量
→
本局变化结构
```

It helps the user observe:

- what kind of inner tendency is meeting the present situation;
- what kind of external force is shaping this round;
- how that relationship may be experienced across the six life interfaces;
- where direction becomes difficult to feel;
- where awareness and Choice may become possible.

Hexagram is not:

- a prediction of future events;
- a verdict of good or bad fortune;
- a permanent personality type;
- a diagnosis of the user;
- a moral evaluation of a response;
- an instruction that must be obeyed;
- the starbeast or Mother Code itself;
- the final Crystal;
- proof that change has already happened;
- a new identity generated by AI;
- a modern scientific law about human psychology.

Hexagram is a symbolic structure for observing change. The symbol serves the person's understanding; it does not receive authority over the person.

## 2. Pattern, Not Fate

“变化规律” means that the product makes a current relationship of forces traceable and repeatable inside its declared symbolic protocol.

It does not mean:

- every person with the same hexagram will experience the same event;
- the system can know what must happen next;
- the current structure determines the user's lifelong direction;
- a cultural symbol is being presented as experimentally proven causal science;
- deterministic calculation proves deterministic destiny.

The product may say:

> 在观爻的符号协议中，这组内外力量形成了这一局卦象。

It must not say:

> 这卦证明你的未来只能如此。

Calculation can be deterministic while lived meaning remains open to recognition, context, and Choice.

## 3. One Round, Not A Permanent Identity

`currentHexagramProfile` is always scoped to “本局”.

It expresses:

> 在当前现实处境中，原始生命倾向与环境力量如何相遇。

It does not express:

> 这个人永远是谁。

The same Mother Code can enter different current hexagrams when the selected environment force changes. The same current hexagram can be experienced differently by different people, at different times, through different primary spaces and Choices.

Therefore:

```text
本局卦象重复出现
≠ 用户没有成长

本局卦象发生变化
≠ 用户已经变成另一个人

卦名保持不变
≠ 这一局没有发生变化
```

The current-round scope must remain visible in copy, presentation, cards, and saved traces.
