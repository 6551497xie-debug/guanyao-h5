# GUANYAO One Light Life Trajectory Protocol

## Status

This is the active GUANYAO One Light semantic protocol under the V2 worldview constitution.

It defines One Light as the continuity of a life moving through time and reality, and locks its relationship to the starbeast, Mother Code, environment force, six-space experience, Gravity, Choice, hexagram, Crystal, Axis Line, legacy trajectory encoding, migration traces, and return-page trajectory.

This is a semantic registration, not a new candidate layer. It does not create a One Light Runtime, insert a new stage into the causal chain, or change current interaction, route, visual, persistence, trajectory, hexagram, or Crystal behavior.

## 1. Locked Definition

> 一光 = 星兽从宇宙时空来到现实世界，并在经历、迷失、觉察与选择中持续展开的生命轨迹。

One Light is not an object placed beside the person. It is the visible continuity of this life itself.

It connects:

```text
出生
→ 经历
→ 迷失
→ 觉察
→ 选择
→ 回归
```

It answers neither “How far has the user progressed?” nor “Which route should the user complete?”

It answers:

> 这个生命怎样一路来到这里，又怎样在此刻重新感到自己仍然可以看见、感受和选择？

One Light is not:

- a task path;
- a progress bar;
- a level, streak, score, or completion percentage;
- a route controller or navigation rail;
- one fixed destiny line;
- the only correct response selected by the system;
- the `AxisLine` component or state machine by itself;
- a behavior sample array or legacy yao-code trajectory;
- one `migrationTrace` or return-page history by itself;
- proof that every experience has been stored;
- a scientific claim that a measurable ray travelled from a star to the user.

One Light is a worldview and expression principle. Existing engineering objects may express one local segment of it without becoming its ontology.

## 2. Continuity, Not Linear Progress

Life continuity does not require uninterrupted forward movement.

One Light may:

- pause;
- bend;
- circle back;
- become difficult to notice;
- split into several possible directions;
- return to an earlier question with new understanding;
- leave a trace without producing an immediate answer.

None of these states means that life has failed, regressed, or lost value.

Therefore:

```text
页面前进
≠ 生命前进

页面退出
≠ 生命轨迹中断

完成更多局
≠ 更高生命等级

保存更多数据
≠ 更完整地拥有自己
```

One Light can provide a sense of continuity across product moments, but the product must not claim to contain the user's complete life.

## 3. The Six Movements

The constitutional sequence describes six life movements, not six mandatory screens.

### Birth

Birth means that a life has entered time and reality.

In the current product, public-calendar input, Chinese-lunar conversion, birth hour, starbeast derivation, and Mother Code calculation may provide symbolic origin context. They do not prove cosmic destiny, measure a literal light source, or reduce the person to birth data.

### Experience

Experience means that life meets the world through body, emotion, thought, action, memory, and motivation.

It is not the passive collection of events by the system. An experience belongs to the person even when GUANYAO cannot classify or store it.

### Lost

Being lost means that reality's pulls, external standards, familiar responses, and uncertainty can make inner direction difficult to feel.

It is not moral failure, damage, weak energy, or a requirement to make the light visually die.

### Awareness

Awareness means that what is happening becomes observable without immediately being judged or corrected.

It is not a system diagnosis and does not require the user to accept every interpretation.

### Choice

Choice means that direction again belongs to the person. The line may continue, pause, widen, or reorient according to the user's recognition.

It is not a forced branch, correct-answer glow, or route-completion command.

### Return

Return means returning to the capacity to feel, observe, interpret, and choose.

It does not mean returning to an original pure state, completing a circular game loop, recovering a system-prescribed answer, or becoming immune to future Gravity.

## 4. One Light Is A Throughline, Not A Main-Chain Stage

The GUANYAO 1.0 main chain remains:

```text
时空调频
→ 母码卡 / 原始生命倾向
→ 压力种子 / 环境力量
→ Gravity / 六维生命体验
→ 当前生命界面 6 节点观察
→ Choice / 重新取得方向
→ 星兽反馈
→ Crystal / 本局生命印记
```

One Light runs through this chain semantically:

```text
一光 / 生命轨迹
├─ 在出生时间中取得起点语境
├─ 以星兽生命原型被辨认
├─ 以母码表达原始生命倾向
├─ 在环境力量与现实引力中发生偏转
├─ 通过六个生命界面被体验
├─ 在觉察中重新变得可见
├─ 在 Choice 中重新取得方向
└─ 在 Crystal 中留下本局生命印记
```

One Light must not be inserted as an additional Runtime candidate, route, readiness gate, domain object, or persistence requirement merely because it is the visual and narrative throughline.

## 5. Relationship To Starbeast

The starbeast is the life being accompanied and recognized. One Light is that life's continuity through time and reality.

The relationship is:

```text
星兽 = 自己
一光 = 这个自己一路经历并持续展开的生命轨迹
```

The light does not leash, summon, charge, feed, control, or rank the starbeast.

A starbeast may stand beside, within, ahead of, or emerge from the light as a visual metaphor, but the composition must preserve that both refer to the same living subject. It must not turn the person into a controller and the starbeast into a dependent game character.

The starbeast's posture may make a current relationship to reality visible. It must not imply that inactivity, refusal, or page exit injures the life archetype or extinguishes its light.

## 6. Relationship To Chrono, Birth Context, And Mother Code

Chrono and birth input provide the current product's origin context.

The established calculation boundaries remain:

```text
公历出生日期
→ 中国农历年月日

农历年月日 + 地支时数
→ 卦以八除
→ 母码 / 下卦
```

The starbeast remains derived from the birth-date-to-lunar-mansion chain defined by its own protocol. Birth location remains geographical context and does not determine the starbeast or Mother Code.

Within One Light semantics:

- birth data marks a symbolic point of arrival;
- Mother Code expresses an original life tendency;
- neither one defines the full future trajectory;
- no later screen should claim that the entire path was already fixed at birth.

“来自宇宙时空的一束生命之光” is worldview language. It does not change the existing calendar algorithms or add a modern astrophysical causality claim.

## 7. Relationship To Environment Force And Gravity

Pressure Seed expresses the environment force being encountered. Gravity expresses how reality's pull acts across the current life experience.

One Light allows those encounters to be presented as part of a living trajectory:

```text
生命正在展开
→ 遇到现实力量
→ 方向受到牵引
→ 某些熟悉反应变得更强
→ 牵引被看见
```

The environment force is not a stain on the line. Gravity is not proof that the light is weak.

Visual tension, occlusion, bending, slowing, dispersion, or orbit change may express a current pull. They must not become a moral meter, damage bar, energy deficiency, or threat that the user's life will disappear if they do not continue.

## 8. Relationship To The Six Spaces And Six Nodes

The six spaces are where one life experiences the world through:

- body;
- emotion;
- thought;
- action;
- memory;
- motivation.

One Light may visually pass through or resonate with these six interfaces. This means that one continuous life is being felt in several ways. It does not mean six separate souls, six scored attributes, or six checkpoints required to keep the light alive.

The six nodes remain the current observation rhythm and Runtime completion boundary. Their completion state must not be presented as the percentage of a user's life trajectory.

```text
六节点完成态
≠ 一光完成态

Primary Petal
≠ 生命唯一入口

节点切换
≠ 生命被系统推进
```

## 9. Relationship To Choice

Choice is where life direction becomes user-owned again.

One Light should make room for:

- a line continuing in a newly recognized direction;
- more than one visible possibility;
- a pause without visual punishment;
- a direction being revised in the user's own words;
- leaving and returning without a failure state;
- declining a system suggestion while keeping the person present.

Choice does not need to look like a dramatic fork. A slight reorientation can be enough.

The system may illuminate possibilities. It must not draw one path as fate and require the user to follow it.

## 10. Relationship To Hexagram

Hexagram expresses how forces meet and how change is structured in one life experience.

One Light expresses the continuity of the life moving through such structures.

Therefore:

```text
一光 = 生命轨迹
卦象 = 变化规律

生命轨迹经过一局卦象
≠ 卦象拥有或决定整个生命轨迹
```

`currentHexagramProfile` remains the current-round structural identity formed from the lower and upper trigrams. It does not become a coordinate that fixes every future direction of One Light.

One Light must not be used to bypass the established formation chain, generate a new hexagram, select a changed line, or predict an ending.

## 11. Relationship To ChangeExperience And Migration Trace

ChangeExperience expresses one structured lived-change unit. `PersonaMigrationImpact` and its `migrationTrace` express the current-round movement from a familiar model toward a newly recognized response.

They are evidence-bearing local traces, not the whole One Light.

```text
一次 ChangeExperience
→ 在一光中发生一次可见偏转

一个 migrationTrace
→ 描述本局变化如何发生

多个 migrationTrace
≠ 系统已经完整记录用户的一生
```

Prepared Runtime material must not be narrated as a lived trajectory before the established recognition, confirmation, and readiness boundaries are satisfied.

## 12. Relationship To Crystal

Crystal is the star imprint left by one life experience.

One Light is not completed by Crystal. Crystal is one trace retained from the path already travelled.

```text
一光经过这一局
→ 现实力量被体验
→ 方向被重新看见或选择
→ Crystal 留下本局生命印记
→ 一光继续
```

A Crystal may be rendered as a point, condensation, refraction, or quiet star along the light. It must not look like loot proving that the user reached the end of the trajectory.

Not forming, viewing, saving, or depositing a Crystal does not break One Light or invalidate the experience.

## 13. Existing Engineering Concepts Must Remain Distinct

The following concepts can carry local trajectory meaning but must not be collapsed into One Light:

| Existing Concept | Current Engineering Role | One Light Boundary |
| --- | --- | --- |
| `AxisLinePage` | Local 1px interaction and state presentation | A physical interaction metaphor, not the whole life trajectory |
| `axisLineSystem` | Event, state-transition, subscription, and optional behavior-asset logic | A local logic system, not a worldview owner or life-history store |
| `AxisTrajectorySample[]` | Samples time and line offset during one run | Interaction data, not the user's lived history |
| `GyAxisLineV2` | Reusable visual-system line component | A renderer, not One Light itself |
| Chrono light track | Visual context for locating birth time | An origin interaction metaphor, not a cosmic proof or full trajectory |
| `autoYaoPath` / `interactiveYaoPath` | Existing five-bit trajectory compatibility data | Code-path data, not life progress |
| `sixthYaoChoice` / `finalChoiceCode` / `choiceHistory` | Legacy yao and final-code representation | Not the user's complete Choice or One Light |
| `BehaviorTrajectory` | Existing hexagram-to-behavior mapping | A semantic mapping type, not a life-history record |
| `migrationTrace` | Current-round model-movement trace | One local change trace, not permanent identity |
| Return trajectory points | Experimental return-page persisted visual history | A bounded UI dataset, not a complete record of life |
| Personality Ring Lite | Explicitly saved current-round Crystal carrier | A revisit surface, not the owner or measure of One Light |

Future code must not create a universal `oneLight` field merely by renaming one of these structures. A formal domain model would require a separate protocol, privacy, migration, ownership, deletion, and cross-round semantics review.

## 14. Axis Line Runtime Boundary

The current `axisLineSystem` remains a local engine that can:

- emit axis events;
- expose state transitions;
- broadcast through named subscriptions;
- sample one interaction run;
- derive an `AxisBehaviorAsset` on demand.

The current boundary also keeps the system persistence-neutral, while `AxisLinePage` hands rebound completion into explicit route state and does not store an orphaned behavior asset.

This protocol does not reinterpret `IDLE`, `TENSION`, `OVERRIDE`, `BREAK`, or `REBOUND` as universal stages of human life.

In particular:

```text
REBOUND_COMPLETE
≠ 回归已经完成

BREAK
≠ 用户的人生破裂

peakTension
≠ 生命受损程度

behaviorTrajectory.samples
≠ 生命经历数量
```

The Axis Line may later become one visual expression of One Light only through a separate implementation knife with accessibility, interaction, state, and semantic review.

## 15. Return And Persistence Boundary

The current return-page trajectory adapter stores a bounded, versioned point array for an experimental visual flow and retains legacy reading compatibility.

That data must not be presented as:

- the user's complete life trajectory;
- a psychological record;
- proof of long-term growth;
- a measure of how much the user has changed;
- the sole source for continuity across sessions.

One Light does not require total persistence to remain meaningful.

Any future cross-round One Light history must separately define:

- what is saved and why;
- whether saving is explicit or automatic;
- user visibility and control;
- deletion and reset behavior;
- privacy and sensitive-context minimization;
- legacy migration;
- whether absence of data means “unknown” rather than “nothing happened”.

This protocol does not authorize that system.

## 17. Visual And Emotional Direction

One Light should feel ancient, quiet, continuous, and alive.

Suitable visual qualities include:

- a line whose presence predates the current screen;
- subtle continuity across scene transitions;
- breathing, bending, refraction, orbit, widening, or quiet return;
- starbeast and light sharing one material or rhythm;
- six-space changes appearing as different experiences of one light;
- Crystal condensing from a path already travelled;
- darkness providing depth without becoming punishment;
- visible alternatives without one path being declared correct.

Avoid:

- progress bars disguised as cosmic rails;
- level maps and checkpoint chains;
- red failure lines or energy-depletion meters;
- a leash between user and starbeast;
- a single route highlighted as destiny;
- forced forward motion that removes pause or return;
- completion explosions that imply life has reached an endpoint;
- making color, brightness, or length a comparative rank between users.

One Light must not rely on vision alone. Any future implementation that carries essential state through line motion, brightness, or color must provide equivalent text, structure, reduced-motion behavior, and accessible interaction feedback.

The emotional objective is:

> 原来无论我怎样绕行、停留或重新选择，这个正在经历生命的自己一直都在。

## 18. AI And Human Sovereignty Boundary

AI may help illuminate a segment of the path, name a possible pattern, or make an alternative direction easier to see.

AI must not:

- claim to know the user's complete trajectory;
- turn prediction into destiny;
- choose a route because it is easier to calculate;
- infer consent from continued navigation;
- use stored history to overrule present self-understanding;
- make the user dependent on the system to feel that their life continues;
- describe missing data as missing life.

The line belongs to the life being lived, not to the model observing it.
