# GUANYAO Genesis Reality Pressure Projection Protocol V1.0

## Purpose

P108 introduces the first external-reality influence after the personal reveal:

```text
Personal Star Beast Reveal
↓
Reality Pressure Reference
↓
Genesis Reality Pressure Projection
↓
Life Presence Projection
↓
Isolated Prototype Renderer
```

The projection expresses how reality begins to press on an already-present
personal life. It is not a new life identity, not a second reveal, and not a
replacement for the formal life-system chain.

## What the pressure projection means

Reality pressure is translated into a small, renderer-neutral vocabulary:

- field compression;
- boundary load;
- core resistance;
- flow deflection;
- temporal weight.

These are expression parameters, not copied pressure facts. The projection
keeps only an opaque formal pressure reference and remains identity-blind.

## Boundary with the life system

The formal chain remains:

```text
Personal Star Beast
↓
Reality Pressure
↓
Gravity
↓
Choice
↓
Crystal
```

P108 does not calculate or replace Gravity, Choice, or Crystal. It does not
write to Life State, Storage, or Production Runtime. It is an isolated visual
observation of pressure entering the presence field, not a shortcut around the
life system.

## Acceptance question

The question is not whether the scene becomes darker or busier. It is:

> 当现实开始施加引力时，用户是否感觉到“它仍在这里，但世界正在触碰它”？

Pressure must remain quiet, structural, and reversible. It must not become a
combat effect, a damage meter, an animal pose, or a UI warning.

The scope remains `ISOLATED_PROTOTYPE_ONLY`.
